Il Papavero Apartment

The 80sqm 'Il Papavero' apartment is the largest. In total 4 beds divided into two rooms, one of which is double, and a bedroom with 2 sofa-bed and TV. Two bathrooms, both with a shower room furnished exclusively by the 'Nucci & Caratelli' company. Large fitted kitchen, equipped with oven, refrigerator and various wall units. Living room with sofa and armchairs, fireplace and television. Fully furnished apartments with antique furniture.
