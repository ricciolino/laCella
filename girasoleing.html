Il Girasole Apartment

The 70sq.m 'Il Girasole' apartment is located in the old farm on two floors. On the first floor, living room with kitchenette, fridge and television. Sofa bed (2 seats) and two armchairs. On the second floor 4 beds divided into a double bedroom, a bedroom with mezzanine and two single beds. A bathroom with a shower room exclusively furnished by the 'Nucci & Caratelli' company. Fully furnished apartments with antique furniture.
