The farmhouse La Cella is located in a splendid panoramic position, in the middle of the Crete Senesi. It is about 2 km from Asciano (the village), and 30 km from Siena. The structure is divided into two parts: the hut and part of an old farm. In the hut we find the largest apartment, Il Papavero. While in the old farm we find a small apartment, Il Girasole.
